# Overview

BattleBot Sparta is a multidisciplinary robotics project focused on the design and development of a heavyweight combat robot. The project combines mechanical engineering, embedded systems, electronics, CAD design, manufacturing, and teamwork to create a robust robot capable of competing in combat robotics events.

The project was initiated by **Sparta Team**, a robotics team co-founded by **Koussay Attaya**, **Wisssem Stanbouli**, and **Radhwan Hamrouni**, with the objective of applying engineering principles to a real-world robotics challenge while developing practical experience in system integration and collaborative engineering.

---

# Project Objectives

- Design a reliable combat robot for robotics competitions.
- Develop a durable mechanical chassis capable of withstanding high-impact collisions.
- Integrate electronic and embedded control systems.
- Design a high-performance rotating weapon system.
- Optimize weight distribution and structural rigidity.
- Improve team collaboration through multidisciplinary engineering.

# Weapon System

One of the major engineering challenges was the design of a high-energy rotating weapon capable of delivering powerful impacts while maintaining structural stability.

The selected concept focused on a rotating disk powered by high-performance brushless motors, requiring careful consideration of motor selection, structural balance, rotational inertia, and safety.

Throughout the design process, different motors and electronic speed controllers (ESCs) were evaluated to identify a configuration capable of meeting the performance requirements of a heavyweight combat robot.
